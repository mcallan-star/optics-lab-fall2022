# week 6 - reflectance / brewster

reflectance off a glass slide vs angle, for s and p polarization.

- slide on a rotation stage, detector catches the reflected beam, polarizer selects s or p
- measured reflected/incident at a bunch of angles
- p-pol reflectance drops to basically ZERO at Brewster's angle. so cool to see live. measured ~56 deg, predicted atan(1.515) = 56.6 deg. matches.

(this is why polarized sunglasses kill glare off water and roads. the glare is mostly horizontally polarized so the glasses just block that axis.)
